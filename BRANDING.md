# innerthought Branding Guidelines

**The Next Interface Is Thought.**

This document outlines the visual identity, color systems, and typography used for the **innerthought** project. Our brand aims to evoke a sense of premium craftsmanship, intellectual depth, and future-forward technology.

---

## 🎨 Color Palette
The brand centered on a "Marble & Gold" aesthetic, utilizing deep earth tones and metallic accents to create a high-end, editorial feel.

| Name | Hex | Variable | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Deep Night** | `#0d0b07` | `--deep` | Primary background, UI surfaces. |
| **Old Gold** | `#c9a84c` | `--gold` | Accents, buttons, brand name, interactive states. |
| **Antique Marble** | `#f5f0e8` | `--marble` | Primary typography, high-contrast text. |
| **Muted Gold** | `#d4b483` | N/A | Secondary text, subtitles, less-critical info. |
| **Terracotta** | `#c17a4a` | `--terracotta` | Atmospheric accents, warmth. |
| **Sage** | `#7a9e7e` | `--sage` | Contrast elements, natural tones. |

---

## 🖋️ Typography
We prioritize timeless, serif fonts that contrast with our cutting-edge technology to create a "modern manuscript" aesthetic.

### **Primary Typeface**
- **Font Stack:** `Georgia, 'Times New Roman', serif`
- **Fallback:** `serif`

### **Hierarchy & Styles**

| Element | Size / Style | Letter Spacing | Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Branding Eyebrow** | `0.75rem` | `0.4em` | UPPERCASE |
| **Primary Headlines** | `2rem` – `4rem` | Normal | Normal |
| **Subtitles** | `1rem` – `1.3rem` | Normal | Sentence Case |
| **Interactive (Buttons)** | `0.85rem` – `0.9rem` | `0.15em` – `0.25em`| UPPERCASE |
| **Footnotes** | `0.7rem` | `0.05em` | Normal |

## 🗣️ Voice & Narrative
The **innerthought** voice is visionary, calm, and sophisticated. We communicate the complexity of subvocal AI with simplicity and poetic focus.

### **Core Identity**
- **The Brand:** innerthought
- **The Hook:** "Thinking and communicating were separate. We're bridging the gap."
- **The Tech:** "Subvocal AI. Intercepted before sound."

### **Key Copy Elements**
- **Primary Tagline:** *"The Next Interface Is Thought"*
- **Vision Statement:** *"We're building the interface — a new way to think with machines."*

---

## ✨ Design Principles
- **Minimalism:** Use generous whitespace and avoid cluttered layouts.
- **Luminosity:** Use subtle glows (`text-shadow`) to make text feel like "light" rather than "ink."
- **Motion:** Interactions should be fluid and slow (long transitions, smooth eases) to match the pace of human thought.
